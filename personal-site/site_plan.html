Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to entertain people with a little submarine game that they can play on the go! The game is simple but fun for the whole family.

Audience

The Target Audience is young adults who have an interest in steam submarines!

Dynamic elements

The elements in Javascript that I will be using in my webpage to make it Dynamic are updating the lines of text on the page to change numbers to accuratly show the stats of my submarine. Additionally I will be using it to detect button presses and holds. I could use an array to keep track of all the values for the submarine. Functions would be inside of that that I can call to edit parts of the sub.
